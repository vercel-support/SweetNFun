Dalgona

24pcs/Pack

A traditional Korean folk candy! Eaters try to trim their way around the outline or picture on the snack without breaking the picture.

Dalgona is a significant element of the Netflix series Squid Game, with a deadly version of the Dalgona challenge being the second game played in the series. The success of the series led to a revival of the candy's popularity in South Korea.
